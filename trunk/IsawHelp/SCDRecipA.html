Reciprocal Lattice Peak Viewer <

This view shows the Peaks in 3D. They can be twisted and turned to get lines( planes).

    There are TWO possible outputs to the invoking program from this Reciprocal viewer
  1. 4 point Selection Result
  2. 3 plane Selection Result
The MODE( 4 point or 3 plane) can be selected in the appropriate checkbox in the menu item under Information
